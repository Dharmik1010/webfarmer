Cotton

Choose pest-resistant varieties. Provide regular irrigation at flowering stage and monitor for bollworms and aphids.

Sugarcane

Plant disease-free setts in well-drained soil. Maintain adequate soil moisture and practice timely earthing-up.

Soybean

Inoculate seeds with Rhizobium culture. Maintain proper row spacing and avoid waterlogging to boost nodulation.

Barley

Sow in well-drained loamy soil. Apply adequate phosphorus and irrigate lightly at tillering and flowering stages.

Groundnut

Use bold, healthy seeds treated with fungicide. Provide gypsum at flowering to enhance pod filling.

Mustard

Maintain optimum plant population by thinning. Apply sulfur fertilizers to improve oil content and yield.

Pulses

Adopt crop rotation to enrich soil. Use biofertilizers and maintain timely irrigation during flowering and pod setting.

Potato

Use disease-free seed tubers. Maintain cool soil moisture and ridge up soil as plants grow for better tuber development.

Tomato

Plant healthy seedlings in well-drained soil. Provide staking, regular irrigation and timely pest management for high yields.
